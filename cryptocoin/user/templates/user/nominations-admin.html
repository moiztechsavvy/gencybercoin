You can reward a certain amount of coins to students when you assign them a specific activity. Negative reward values are allowed. If you assign the same activity to the same person more than once, the reward amount will be accumulated accordingly.

Hold down "Ctrl" (or "Command" on Mac) to select multiple entries. Hold down "Shift" or click and drag your mouse over the entries to select a range of entries.

Select students from the left and activities/achievements from the right. Then click on "Nominate". You can also select/unselect all by clicking on the corresponding buttons.

Students will be able to see the activities/achievements that have been assigned to them under the Achievements section on the Account page.

You can nominate yourself for activities/achievements to test it out and see how it looks.

Groups

If you have students split up into groups, it may be helpful to group them here for easier activity, achievement, or badge nomination (grouping isn't necessary, but can be convenient). The group that you assigned yourself to will not be collapsed by default, whereas every other group will be.
